- Ability to work effectively within a team and communicate clearly.
- Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot quickly and efficiently.


**Preferred Qualifications:**


- Experience in SEO optimization and performance analysis.
- Familiarity with design software like Adobe Photoshop or Sketch.
- Experience managing e-commerce projects.
- Certifications or training in e-commerce web development.
